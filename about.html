About Drew

I got into coding because I was at a career crossroads. I'd worked in a number of roles and hadn't found something I was truly passionate about. Working for software companies in sales and account management roles, I'd always been curious to have a better understanding of the technology behind the software I was talking with customers about. I've always enjoyed solving problems and took the plunge to start DigitalCrafts, a full stack web development bootcamp in February of 2022.
